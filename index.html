The usually deep purple roots of beetroot are eaten either grilled, boiled, or roasted as a cooked vergetable, cold as a salad after cooking and adding oil and vinegar, or raw and shredded, either alone or combined with any salad vegetable

In fact, Jum Halpert once said this about beets, "Fact, bears eat beets. Bears, beets, Battlestar Galactica".

I have some weird habits. For instance, I love beets. Show me a salad bar and I will clean them out of their beets. — Chris Pratt
